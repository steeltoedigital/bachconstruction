A Dynamic Community Steeped in History

Marietta, Georgia is a city rich in history, dating back to its founding in 1834. The city played a pivotal role during the Civil War, and its historical significance is preserved in numerous museums, historic homes, and landmarks. The Marietta Square, the heart of the city, is encircled by historic buildings, boutique shops, and a variety of restaurants, offering a charming and welcoming atmosphere.

The community in Marietta, GA is known for its strong sense of togetherness. Residents enjoy a range of events and festivals throughout the year, including the annual Marietta StreetFest, the Taste of Marietta event, and the Glover Park Music Series. These events foster a sense of belonging and provide opportunities for residents to connect with one another.

Top-Quality Education Options

For families considering a move to the Marietta area, the quality of education is often a top priority. Marietta is served by both the Marietta City Schools district and the Cobb County Schools, both of which are well-regarded in the state. Marietta High School, for example, is known for its robust International Baccalaureate (IB) program, offering students a rigorous and globally recognized curriculum.

In addition to public schools, the Marietta area is home to several prestigious private schools, such as Walker School and Mount Paran Christian School, which provide alternative education options for families. For higher education, Kennesaw State University, located nearby, offers a wide range of bachelor's and master's programs, making the Marietta area an excellent choice for lifelong learners.

A Thriving and Diverse Economic Environment

Marietta, Georgia boasts a robust and varied economy, which is a significant factor for those considering relocation. The city benefits from its proximity to Atlanta, providing residents with access to a broad range of job opportunities in various industries, including healthcare, education, tech industries, and manufacturing.

Lockheed, one of the largest employers in the area, has a significant presence in Marietta, GA, contributing to the city’s economic stability. Additionally, the WellStar Health Network, another major employer, offers a range of healthcare services and job opportunities. The city's thriving economy is complemented by a low unemployment rate, making this area an attractive destination for professionals seeking career growth.

Close to Atlanta with Excellent Transportation

Cultural Richness and Recreational Opportunities

Marietta, GA is a city that values culture and the arts.
