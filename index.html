When it comes to building your dream home, finding an affordable custom home builder is key. There are several factors to consider when making this important decision.

First and foremost, you'll want to research the reputation of the builder. Look for reviews from past clients and ask for references to get a sense of the quality of their work. A reputable builder will have a portfolio of completed projects that they can show you.

Cost is another important factor to consider. While affordability is key, be wary of builders who offer prices that seem too good to be true. Make sure the builder provides a detailed quote outlining all costs involved in the project.

Experience is also crucial when choosing a custom home builder. Look for a builder with years of experience in the industry and a proven track record of delivering high-quality homes on time and within budget.

Communication is key throughout the building process, so choose a builder who is responsive and transparent in their communication with you. You'll want someone who will listen to your needs and ideas and keep you updated on the progress of your home build.

Lastly, make sure the builder is properly licensed and insured. This will protect you in case anything goes wrong during the construction process.

By considering these factors when choosing an affordable custom home builder, you can ensure that your dream home becomes a reality without breaking the bank.

A Thriving Community with Rich History

Marietta, Georgia is a city steeped in history, dating back to its founding in 1834. The city played a pivotal role during the Civil War, and its rich history is preserved in numerous museums, historic homes, and landmarks. The Marietta Square, the heart of the city, is encircled by historic buildings, boutique shops, and a variety of restaurants, offering a charming and welcoming atmosphere.

The community in Marietta, GA is known for its deep sense of belonging. Residents enjoy a range of events and festivals throughout the year, including the annual Marietta StreetFest, Taste of Marietta, and the Glover Park Music Series. These events foster a sense of belonging and provide opportunities for residents to connect with one another.

Outstanding Educational Resources

For families considering a move to the Marietta area, the quality of education is often a top priority. Marietta is served by both the Marietta City Schools and the Cobb County School District, both of which are well-regarded in the state. Marietta High School, for example, is known for its strong International Baccalaureate (IB) program, offering students a demanding and globally recognized curriculum.

In addition to public schools, Marietta is home to several prestigious private schools, such as Walker School and Mount Paran Christian School, which provide alternative education options for families. For higher education, KSU, located nearby, offers a wide range of bachelor's and master's programs, making the Marietta area an excellent choice for lifelong learners.

Robust and Varied Economic Landscape

Marietta, GA boasts a strong and diverse economy, which is a significant factor for those considering relocation. The city benefits from its proximity to Atlanta, providing residents with access to a broad range of job opportunities in various industries, including medical services, academia, tech industries, and industrial production.

Lockheed Martin, one of the largest employers in the area, has a significant presence in the Marietta region, contributing to the city’s economic stability. Additionally, the WellStar Health System, another major employer, offers a range of healthcare services and job opportunities. The city's thriving economy is complemented by a low unemployment rate, making this area an attractive destination for professionals seeking career growth.
