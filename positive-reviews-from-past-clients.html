The community in Marietta, GA is known for its tight-knit community. Residents enjoy a range of events and festivals throughout the year, including the annual Marietta StreetFest, the Taste of Marietta event, and the Glover Park Concert Series. Top-Quality Education Options

For families considering a move to Marietta, GA, the quality of education is often a top priority.
