A Thriving Community with Rich History

Marietta, Georgia is a city steeped in history, dating back to its founding in 1834. The city played a crucial role during the Civil War, and its storied past is preserved in numerous museums, historic homes, and landmarks. The historic Marietta Square, the heart of the city, is encircled by historic buildings, boutique shops, and a variety of restaurants, offering a charming and welcoming atmosphere.

The community in Marietta, GA is known for its tight-knit community. Residents enjoy a range of events and festivals throughout the year, including the Marietta StreetFest, Taste of Marietta, and the Glover Park Concert Series. These events promote community connection and provide opportunities for residents to connect with one another.

Outstanding Educational Resources

For families considering a move to the Marietta area, the quality of education is often a top priority. Marietta is served by both the Marietta City Schools district and the Cobb County Schools, both of which are respected in the state. The local high school, for example, is known for its strong International Baccalaureate (IB) program, offering students a demanding and globally recognized curriculum.

In addition to public schools, Marietta is home to several prestigious private schools, such as Walker School and Mount Paran Christian School, which provide alternative education options for families. For higher education, Kennesaw State University, located nearby, offers a wide range of bachelor's and master's programs, making Marietta an excellent choice for lifelong learners.

Robust and Varied Economic Landscape

Marietta, GA boasts a robust and varied economy, which is a significant factor for those considering relocation. The city benefits from its proximity to Atlanta, providing residents with access to a broad range of job opportunities in various industries, including medical services, academia, tech industries, and manufacturing.

Lockheed, one of the largest employers in the area, has a significant presence in Marietta, contributing to the city’s economic stability. Additionally, the WellStar Health Network, another major employer, offers a range of healthcare services and job opportunities. The city's thriving economy is complemented by a low unemployment rate, making this area an attractive destination for professionals seeking career growth.

Proximity to Atlanta and Transportation

Marietta, Georgia offers the perfect balance of peaceful suburban life and urban convenience. Vibrant Cultural and Outdoor Activities
