A Dynamic Community Steeped in History

Marietta, Georgia is a city with a deep historical background, dating back to its founding in 1834. The city played a crucial role during the Civil War, and its historical significance is preserved in numerous museums, historic homes, and landmarks. The historic Marietta Square, the heart of the city, is anchored by historic buildings, boutique shops, and a variety of restaurants, offering a charming and welcoming atmosphere.

The community in Marietta, GA is known for its strong sense of togetherness. Residents enjoy a range of events and festivals throughout the year, including the annual Marietta StreetFest, Taste of Marietta, and the Glover Park Concert Series. These events encourage community engagement and provide opportunities for residents to connect with one another.

Excellent Education Opportunities

For families considering a move to the Marietta area, the quality of education is often a top priority. Marietta is served by both the Marietta City Schools district and the Cobb County School District, both of which are highly regarded in the state. Marietta High School, for example, is known for its strong International Baccalaureate (IB) program, offering students a demanding and globally recognized curriculum.

In addition to public schools, the Marietta area is home to several prestigious private schools, such as The Walker School and Mount Paran Christian School, which provide alternative education options for families. For higher education, KSU, located nearby, offers a wide range of bachelor's and master's programs, making the Marietta area an excellent choice for lifelong learners.

A Strong and Diverse Economy

Marietta, Georgia boasts a robust and varied economy, which is a significant factor for those considering relocation. The city benefits from its proximity to Atlanta, providing residents with access to a broad range of job opportunities in various industries, including medical services, academia, technology, and manufacturing.

Lockheed, one of the largest employers in the area, has a significant presence in Marietta, GA, contributing to the city’s economic stability. Additionally, the WellStar Health System, another major employer, offers a range of healthcare services and job opportunities. The city's thriving economy is complemented by a low unemployment rate, making Marietta, GA an attractive destination for professionals seeking career growth.

Proximity to Atlanta and Transportation
